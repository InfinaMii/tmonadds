✧The Terramon Expansion is a side-mod focusing on expanding the content in the Terramon mod!

✧ This includes:
• Seven GameBoy consoles which can play tracks from FireRed!
• An advanced Pokemon Mini, capable of streaming unique audio in each biome!
• Two new vanity costumes to dress up as your favourite chatacters!
• A Ride Pager, allowing you to ride 
• Haunted plush dolls which are said to have transformative properties...
